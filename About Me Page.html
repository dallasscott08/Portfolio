About Me

I recently received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from St. John's University in Queens, New York. While attending St. John's, I worked as the Business Manager for The Torch, which is the student newspaper, for my sophomore and junior years. I am currently looking for an entry level Software Engineering position and am willing to relocate if necessary. I would like to join an organization that offers opportunity for personal growth and advancement.

Ever since my parents bought me my first computer, I have had an interest in computers. It is because of this that I decided to go to college and get a degree in Computer Science.

Other than programming, some other things I enjoy are video games, working out at the gym, traveling, watching television, playing guitar and gourmet cooking. I constantly look for ways to get my assigned tasks done faster and more efficiently. I was born in 1990 in Silver Springs, Maryland but I currently live in Hinsdale, Illinois.
